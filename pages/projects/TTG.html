Project: Tidal Turbine Gearbox for Power Generation


This is the design of a gearbox outfitted for an Andritz Hydro Hammerfest MK1 1500 kW tidal turbine; the gearbox design serves to convert the kinetic energy of tidal flow into mechanical input for an electric generator

To transfer the kinetic energy of the tidal current, a gearbox comprised of three planetary stages and one helical gear stage was implemented within the turbine system.
