Project: Ergonomic Under-Desk Bike


This project was a comprehensive, collaborative project intended to create an energy-harvesting solution within an ergonomic, under-desk bicycle. The bicycle is to be used in office workspaces by those who want to cater to their physical health while they work.

There is no need for messy wires, the bicycle charges itself while the user pedals: it was designed to produce 5V at 30rpm, enough to keep the battery charged at all times. The drive belt is in tension with a small pulley that turns a ~300 kv motor. There's an interfaceable mobile app, and a port for mobile device charging.


The CAD for this project is available upon request.

Skills: Design, 3D Modelling, Power Generation, Machining
